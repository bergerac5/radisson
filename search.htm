          Hotel details
          A 3-minute walk from Kigali Convention Centre, this modern, high-end hotel is 5 km from Kigali International Airport. … MORE


          Streamlined rooms provide complimentary Wi-Fi, flat-screens and balconies. Upgraded rooms and suites add Nespresso machines, living rooms, extra bedrooms, and/or access to a club lounge with free breakfast and snacks. There's 24-hour room service.

          Parking is free. A breakfast buffet (fee) is served in an airy restaurant. There's also an Italian eatery with a terrace. Other amenities include a bar, an outdoor pool and a gym, plus a spa area with a sauna and a steam room. A business center and an airport shuttle are available.
